ETC TUBE


Evacuated glass tubes used as the absorber in ETC Solar Water heaters. The ETC is having two concentric tubes with vacuum between the tubes. The outer tube is transparent allowing light rays to pass through with reflection. The inner tube of outer surface is coated with (Al-N-Al) for excellent solar radiation. The heat inside the glass tube will not transferred to the outer tube because of vacuum present in between.
